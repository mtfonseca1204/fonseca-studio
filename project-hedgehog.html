Project Description

This project consists of the institutional landing page of Hedgehog, a Web3 initiative focused on developing an on-chain Prediction Market application. The main goal of the page is to open a waitlist for the product, which is expected to be launched in 2026, while also presenting the project’s proposal, vision, and positioning.

The landing page serves as the first point of contact between Hedgehog, its future users, and partners, helping communicate what is being built and the next steps of the project. The focus is on generating interest, validating demand, and gathering users interested in the launch of the final product.
